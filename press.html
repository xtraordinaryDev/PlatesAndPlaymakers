OFFICIAL BOILERPLATE

Plates & Playmakers is an annual luxury charity dining event held in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The inaugural event takes place on Thursday, July 9, 2026 at Quincy Hall in the North Loop neighborhood. Twelve of the Twin Cities' most celebrated chefs — including multiple James Beard Award winners and nominees — gather to cook for 400 guests in support of families at M Health Fairview Masonic Children's Hospital. The event supports four core programs: on-site food pantries, cafeteria credits, bedside meals, and grocery assistance. Fund allocation details to be announced.
