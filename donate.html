THE IMPACT

WHERE EVERY DOLLAR GOES

FOOD PANTRIES

On-site pantries stocked with shelf-stable staples, fresh produce, and culturally appropriate foods. Available to any family at any time — no questions asked.

CAFETERIA CREDITS

Daily meal allowances for caregivers who live at the hospital alongside their child. A simple solution to a heartbreaking problem.

BEDSIDE MEALS

For families who can't leave the room, food comes to them. A warm meal delivered to the bedside of a sick child — and the person who refuses to leave.

GROCERY ASSISTANCE

When children go home, families often go home depleted. Grocery assistance bridges the gap so a child's recovery can happen in a household that's fully nourished.
