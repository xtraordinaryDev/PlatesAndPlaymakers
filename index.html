It started in 1992 as Taste of the Tours—a beloved Twin Cities tradition that brought neighbors together around great food and a greater cause. Over three decades and 100 million meals, that spirit has grown. Plates & Playmakers is the rebrand built to centralize and accelerate the mission: tackling hunger in Minneapolis and St. Paul. Every ticket strengthens three food banks keeping our neighbors fed—weekend kids' bags, neighborhood food shelves, and emergency response when families need it most. A dedicated slice of proceeds also supports independent restaurants and hospitality so small kitchens can keep showing up for their blocks.
