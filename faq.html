THE EVENT

What is Plates & Playmakers?

Plates & Playmakers is an annual luxury charity dining event that brings together 12 of the Twin Cities' most celebrated chefs for one unforgettable evening. Guests enjoy unlimited tastings, curated wine and spirits pairings, and live programming — all in support of families at M Health Fairview Masonic Children's Hospital.

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes unlimited tastings from all 12 chef stations, curated wine and spirits pairings throughout the evening, full access to the venue and all programming, and the knowledge that 100% of net proceeds go directly to families in need at Masonic Children's Hospital.

How long does the evening last?

Doors open at 7:00 PM. The evening runs until approximately 10:30 PM. We encourage you to arrive early and savor every station — there's no rush. Take your time. This is the kind of evening worth lingering in.

Is this a seated dinner?

No — Plates & Playmakers is an elegant tasting format. Guests move freely between 12 chef stations throughout the evening. Cocktail-style seating areas and lounge spaces are available throughout Quincy Hall for those who want to rest between courses.

What is the dress code?

Black tie is encouraged. We want this evening to feel as special as it is — so dress the part. That said, we welcome anyone who dresses with intention. Come hungry, come curious, and come ready to be amazed.

TICKETS

How do I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased through the link on our website homepage. Availability is extremely limited — Quincy Hall is an intimate venue, and we cap attendance to ensure a premium experience for every guest. We strongly encourage early reservation.

Are tickets refundable?

Tickets are non-refundable, as all proceeds go directly to the cause. However, your ticket is fully transferable — simply contact us with the new guest's name and contact information prior to July 9. We're happy to make the change.

Can I purchase a table for a group?

Yes. Tables for groups of 8 or 10 are available through our sponsorship packages, which also include additional event benefits and brand recognition. For table availability and pricing, reach out to us directly at hello@platesandplaymakers.com.

Is there an age requirement?

Plates & Playmakers is a 21+ event. Valid ID will be required at check-in. There are no exceptions — please plan accordingly.

VENUE & LOGISTICS

Where is Quincy Hall?

Quincy Hall is located at 400 N 1st Ave, Minneapolis, MN 55401 — in the heart of the North Loop neighborhood. It is easily accessible from downtown Minneapolis, with several nearby hotels if you're making a night of it (which we highly recommend).

Is parking available?

Yes. The North Loop area has several public parking ramps and surface lots within easy walking distance of Quincy Hall. We recommend using a rideshare service for the most seamless experience — it's an evening of wine and spirits, after all.

Is the venue ADA accessible?

Yes. Quincy Hall is fully ADA accessible. If you have specific accessibility needs or requests, please contact us in advance at hello@platesandplaymakers.com and we will ensure you have the best possible experience.

Can the chefs accommodate dietary restrictions?

Our 12 chefs represent a wide range of cuisines and techniques — there will be considerable variety across all stations. While we cannot guarantee options for every dietary restriction, many stations will offer vegetarian-friendly selections. Please note that our environment is not allergen-free. For specific concerns, contact us in advance.

THE CAUSE

Where do ticket proceeds go?

One hundred percent of net proceeds from Plates & Playmakers benefit families at M Health Fairview Masonic Children's Hospital. Funds are administered by the M Health Fairview Foundation and directed specifically to food assistance programs — including on-site food pantries, cafeteria credits, bedside meals, and grocery assistance for families in need.

Can I donate without attending?

Absolutely. Visit our Donate page to make a direct gift to the cause. No ticket required, no minimum. Any amount makes a real difference for a real family.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Direct donations to M Health Fairview Masonic Children's Hospital are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes. For ticket purchases, a portion of your ticket may be deductible — please consult your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.

How are funds distributed?

Funds raised through Plates & Playmakers are administered by the M Health Fairview Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All funds are directed specifically to food security programs serving families at Masonic Children's Hospital. We maintain full transparency on fund allocation and publish an impact report following each event.
