THE MISSION

LET'S FORK
HUNGER.

No parent or caregiver should wonder how they'll afford their next meal while their child is receiving medical care. When a family is in the hospital, the stress of food insecurity compounds an already devastating experience.

Plates & Playmakers exists to close that gap. Every dollar raised is directed to food assistance programs at M Health Fairview Masonic Children's Hospital — ensuring families can focus on what matters most: being there for their child.

"Food is medicine.
Access to it
should never be
a barrier."
WHERE THE MONEY GOES

FOUR PROGRAMS.
ONE FAMILY AT A TIME.

FOOD PANTRIES

A stocked pantry on-site at Masonic Children's Hospital means families never have to leave the building hungry. Funds keep the shelves filled with shelf-stable staples, fresh produce, and culturally appropriate foods — available to any family, no questions asked.

CAFETERIA CREDITS

Hospital stays are long. Families who live at their child's bedside for days or weeks need access to real, nourishing meals without worrying about cost. Cafeteria credits provide a daily allowance so caregivers can eat — and keep their strength up for what matters most.

BEDSIDE MEALS

For parents and caregivers who can't leave their child's room, bedside meal delivery brings food directly to the family. A warm meal delivered by a kind hand is a small act of care that makes an enormous difference during the hardest days of a family's life.

GROCERY ASSISTANCE

Not all families live close to the hospital. When a child returns home, the financial strain of a long hospitalization often leaves families without the resources for basic groceries. Grocery assistance bridges that gap — so a child's recovery can happen in a home that's fully nourished.

OUR PARTNER

M HEALTH FAIRVIEW
MASONIC CHILDREN'S
HOSPITAL

One of the region's premier pediatric hospitals, Masonic Children's Hospital cares for Minnesota's sickest children — and the families who love them. Located in Minneapolis, it serves thousands of patients every year, many of whom travel far from home and stay for extended periods.

All funds raised through Plates & Playmakers are administered by the M Health Fairview Foundation and directed specifically to food security programs for families at Masonic Children's Hospital.
